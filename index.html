    My Projects

    Optimization of Wall Dimension by Artificial Neural Network Approach.
    Jan 2022 - Jan 2024

    Project Focus: Optimization of retaining wall sections using Graviloft technique and Artificial Neural Network (ANN) models.
    Methodology: Creation of design templates, data generation for Factor of Safety (FOS) and construction costs, soil properties assessment with Plaxis software, and development of ANN models using Excel and Mallub.
    Objective: To identify the optimal retaining wall section based on stability (FOS) and cost-effectiveness.
    Outcome: Establishment of a systematic approach for retaining wall design optimization, providing insights for selecting the most suitable wall section.
    Future Scope: Refinement of ANN models, inclusion of additional parameters for comprehensive analysis, and broader application of the Graviloft technique to diverse soil conditions and project scenarios.

    Use of Waste Material in Construction Industry
    Jan 2021

    Objective: Our project aims to revolutionize the construction industry by utilizing waste materials to create sustainable solutions, addressing environmental concerns and infrastructure needs simultaneously.
    Materials: We repurpose a variety of waste materials, including plastic, coconut shells, demolished debris, rubber, and fly ash, to develop innovative construction materials.
    Innovative Solutions: Our solutions include paver blocks made from recycled plastic, bituminous roads reinforced with plastic as a binding material, utilization of coconut shells as aggregates, incorporation of demolished waste as filler material, and integration of rubber and tire waste as aggregates and fillers in road construction.
    Impact: By reducing waste and enhancing the durability and performance of construction projects, our approach contributes to a greener and more resilient built environment.
    Vision: We envision a future where sustainable construction practices are the norm, leading to long-term environmental benefits and a more sustainable infrastructure landscape.
